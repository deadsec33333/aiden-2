Peggy, the UK’s beloved "ugliest dog" and unlikely Hollywood star, has tragically passed away after being hit by a car outside a McDonald's. Peggy, a mix of Pug and Chinese Crested, captured hearts worldwide with her crooked smile, unruly hair, and constantly protruding tongue, winning her the title of the UK’s Ugliest Dog in 2023.

Peggy’s owner, Holly Middleton, shared the heartbreaking news, expressing the immense loss felt by the family. "We’re devastated," Holly said. "Peggy was so much more than her looks — she was our quirky, lovable companion. She brought us constant laughter and joy."

Peggy’s rise to fame took an unexpected turn when her unique appearance caught Hollywood’s eye. After her win, she joined the Deadpool cast, making a surreal red-carpet debut. Dressed in her "Dogpool" superhero costume, Peggy even enjoyed some snuggles from actor Ryan Reynolds, who was charmed by her sweet personality. Despite her brush with stardom, Peggy remained a regular village dog who loved cuddles on the couch and snuggles in her bed.

The sudden loss of Peggy has left her family and fans heartbroken. Her lovable personality and charm transformed the public’s perception, showing that beauty comes in all forms. Peggy will be dearly missed, and her legacy as the world’s unlikeliest star will live on.
